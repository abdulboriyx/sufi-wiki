# Sufism Wiki Index

## Sources
- [schimmel-mystical-dimensions](wiki/sources/schimmel-mystical-dimensions) — Annemarie Schimmel, *Mystical Dimensions of Islam* (1975)
- [chittick-sufi-path-knowledge](wiki/sources/chittick-sufi-path-knowledge) — William Chittick, *The Sufi Path of Knowledge* (1989)

## Figures
- [rabia-al-adawiyya](wiki/figures/rabia-al-adawiyya) — Divine love; 8th-century ascetic of Basra
- [al-hallaj](wiki/figures/al-hallaj) — Martyr of *Ana al-Haqq*; intoxicated Sufism
- [al-ghazzali](wiki/figures/al-ghazzali) — Synthesis of law and mysticism; *Ihya' 'Ulum al-Din*
- [ibn-arabi](wiki/figures/ibn-arabi) — *Wahdat al-Wujud*; The Greatest Master
- [rumi](wiki/figures/rumi) — Poet of divine love; founder of the Mevleviyya

## Concepts
- [fana-baqa](wiki/concepts/fana-baqa) — Annihilation and Subsistence
- [wahdat-al-wujud](wiki/concepts/wahdat-al-wujud) — Unity of Being (Ibn 'Arabi)
- [imagination](wiki/concepts/imagination) — *Khayal*: the intermediate world of images
- [nur-muhammadi](wiki/concepts/nur-muhammadi) — The Muhammadan Light; The Perfect Man (*Insan Kamil*)
- [tawakkul](wiki/concepts/tawakkul) — Absolute Trust in God

## Practices
- [dhikr](wiki/practices/dhikr) — Remembrance/Recollection of God
- [sama](wiki/practices/sama) — Mystical audition; spiritual concert
- [adab](wiki/practices/adab) — Etiquette of the Path

## Orders
- [qadiriyya](wiki/orders/qadiriyya) — Founded by 'Abd al-Qadir al-Jilani; most widespread order
- [chishtiyya](wiki/orders/chishtiyya) — Dominant order of South Asia; sama and qawwali
- [mevleviyya](wiki/orders/mevleviyya) — Order of Rumi; the whirling dervishes
- [naqshbandiyya](wiki/orders/naqshbandiyya) — Silent dhikr; Shari'a-oriented; Central Asian origin

## Debates
- [intoxication-vs-sobriety](wiki/debates/intoxication-vs-sobriety) — *Sukr* vs. *Sahw*: the two poles of the mystic path
- [sama-controversy](wiki/debates/sama-controversy) — Permissibility and interpretation of spiritual music
