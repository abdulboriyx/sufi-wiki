# Sufism Wiki Index

## Sources
- [[schimmel-mystical-dimensions]] — Annemarie Schimmel, *Mystical Dimensions of Islam* (1975)

## Figures
- [[rabia-al-adawiyya]] — Divine love; 8th-century ascetic of Basra
- [[al-hallaj]] — Martyr of *Ana al-Haqq*; intoxicated Sufism
- [[al-ghazzali]] — Synthesis of law and mysticism; *Ihya' 'Ulum al-Din*
- [[ibn-arabi]] — *Wahdat al-Wujud*; The Greatest Master
- [[rumi]] — Poet of divine love; founder of the Mevleviyya

## Concepts
- [[fana-baqa]] — Annihilation and Subsistence
- [[wahdat-al-wujud]] — Unity of Being (Ibn 'Arabi)
- [[nur-muhammadi]] — The Muhammadan Light; The Perfect Man (*Insan Kamil*)
- [[tawakkul]] — Absolute Trust in God

## Practices
- [[dhikr]] — Remembrance/Recollection of God
- [[sama]] — Mystical audition; spiritual concert
- [[adab]] — Etiquette of the Path

## Orders
- [[qadiriyya]] — Founded by 'Abd al-Qadir al-Jilani; most widespread order
- [[chishtiyya]] — Dominant order of South Asia; sama and qawwali
- [[mevleviyya]] — Order of Rumi; the whirling dervishes
- [[naqshbandiyya]] — Silent dhikr; Shari'a-oriented; Central Asian origin

## Debates
- [[intoxication-vs-sobriety]] — *Sukr* vs. *Sahw*: the two poles of the mystic path
- [[sama-controversy]] — Permissibility and interpretation of spiritual music
